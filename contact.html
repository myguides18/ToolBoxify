Best way to reach the project

For bugs or feature requests, open an issue in the GitHub repository for this site: github.com/myguides18/ToolBoxify/issues.

What to include

  • The tool you were using, such as Image Compressor, PDF Tools, or Format Converter.
  • Your browser and device type.
  • A short description of what happened and what you expected.

Do not send private files unless you are comfortable sharing them publicly in a GitHub issue.
