PNG, JPEG, and WEBP are all common image formats, but they are not interchangeable for every job.

PNG

PNG is often best for screenshots, interface graphics, logos, and images that need transparency. File sizes can be larger for photos.

JPEG

JPEG is widely compatible and usually works well for photos. It does not support transparency and can show artifacts at low quality settings.

WEBP

WEBP often produces smaller files for web use and supports modern compression. It is widely supported in current browsers, but JPEG and PNG may still be safer for older workflows.
